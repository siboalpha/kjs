About Us

We have a dream to make Rwanda a better place.

KJS is owned and managed by four professional tour guides. Their idea was to allow tourists to experience local life, meet local people and get to learn about the culture. With our programs we support the local community that participates in our activities, as well as provide jobs for the young people in our community.
